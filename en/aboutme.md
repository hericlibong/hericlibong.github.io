My name is **Héric Libong**. I build **data stories**: narratives driven by numbers, designed to be **read like a story**. My playground is data analysis and visualization applied to concrete topics (sports, current affairs, social issues), with a strong focus on clarity, storytelling, and design. I enjoy transforming raw tables into visual readings that are simple, understandable, and open to discussion.

On this website, you'll find my **articles**, **projects**, and **resources**: interactive visualizations (D3.js, Flourish, Datawrapper), notebooks and scripts (Python, SQL), and methodological notes on how to move from a question to a dataset, then to a visualization that "speaks." The goal is twofold: share publishable explorations and document a reproducible process—from the initial idea to the final dataviz.
